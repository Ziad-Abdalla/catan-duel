# Intrigue + Merchant Princes — per-card doability audit

Every card is playable in the trust-based hot-seat. Effects resolve via the card's
*Resolve effect…* panel (guided one-click steps where listed, plus the always-present
universal toolkit: gain/steal/give resources, adjust VP & points, grant/draw cards,
move & remove placed cards). The card art shows the authoritative printed rules.
