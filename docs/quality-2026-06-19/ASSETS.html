Catan Duel — optional audio & art

Everything already works with zero downloads (sound is synthesized, art is drawn). These are optional upgrades. Pick a file, rename it to the slot name, drop it in the repo — or just download them into one folder and send it over and I'll wire them in.

How to use
  • Click a link, preview, and download the track/file you like.
  • Rename it to the exact slot filename and put it in public/audio/ (e.g. overwrite bgm-1.mp3). That's it — it loads automatically, no code change.
  • Prefer the CC0 / Pixabay options — they need no credit and are safe to commit to the shared repo. If you pick a CC-BY track (e.g. Incompetech), tell me and I'll add the required one-line credit.
